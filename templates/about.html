About Gram Tarakki Foundation

Empowering rural communities through education, health and sustainable livelihood initiatives.

Our Mission

To empower marginalized and rural communities by providing access to education, healthcare, skill development and self-reliant opportunities.

We believe in creating sustainable change by addressing the root causes of poverty and inequality through collaborative, community-driven initiatives.

Our Vision

Our Journey

Gram Tarakki Foundation was founded with the belief that grassroots change creates lasting impact. Starting with small community programs, the foundation gradually expanded into education, karate training, yoga, health awareness and skill development initiatives across villages.

Over the years, we've reached thousands of individuals, transforming lives through our holistic approach to community development.
