OUR PROGRAMS

Creating meaningful change through education, health, skill development and community training.

Gram Tarakki Foundation runs multiple community-focused programs designed to empower individuals, promote wellbeing and build self-reliant villages.

Karate & Self-defence

This program focuses on physical fitness, discipline, confidence building and self-defence training for children and women.

  • Regular training sessions
  • Belt examination & certification
  • Stage performances & demonstrations
  • Guidance by certified instructors

Education

Providing quality education support to rural children through learning resources, guidance and awareness programs.

  • After-school tutoring programs
  • Digital literacy and computer training
  • Library and reading initiatives
  • Scholarship opportunities for meritorious students
Know More

Health

Ensuring accessible healthcare services and health awareness programs for rural communities.

  • Regular health check-up camps
  • Nutrition awareness programs
  • First-aid training workshops
  • Mental health awareness sessions
Know More

Skill Training

Vocational training to enhance employability and self-sufficiency among youth and women.

  • Tailoring and embroidery training
  • Computer and digital skills
  • Handicraft and artisan training
  • Entrepreneurship development programs
Know More

Yoga

Promoting physical and mental wellbeing through regular yoga sessions and meditation practices.

  • Daily morning yoga sessions
  • Yoga for stress management
  • Therapeutic yoga for specific health conditions
  • Yoga certification programs
Know More

Children & Elders

Special care and support programs for vulnerable age groups including children and senior citizens.

  • Day care facilities for children
  • Elderly companionship programs
  • Nutrition support for malnourished children
  • Health monitoring for senior citizens

Animal Welfare

Providing care and medical support for animals in rural areas and promoting animal rights awareness.

  • Animal health check-up camps
  • Vaccination drives for livestock
  • Shelter and care for stray animals
  • Animal rights awareness programs

Environment

Tree plantation, waste management and environmental awareness campaigns for sustainable living.

  • Tree plantation drives
  • Plastic waste reduction initiatives
  • Rainwater harvesting projects
  • Environmental education in schools
