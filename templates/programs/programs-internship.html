About the Program

Gram Tarakki Foundation's Internship Program is designed to bridge the gap between education and professional experience. We provide aspiring students and young professionals with meaningful opportunities to develop skills, build networks and contribute to impactful community projects.

Our program offers two distinct tracks — Technical and Non-Technical — ensuring that interns from all academic backgrounds can find a role that aligns with their interests and career goals.

Interns work alongside experienced mentors on real projects, gaining practical knowledge that enhances their resumes and prepares them for the professional world. Duration ranges from 1 to 6 months, with flexible scheduling options.
