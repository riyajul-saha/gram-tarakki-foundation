About the Program

Yoga training is a structured program that combines physical postures (asanas), breathing techniques (pranayama), meditation, and relaxation to improve overall health and well-being. It is suitable for people of all ages, including beginners, students, professionals, and seniors. Regular practice helps increase flexibility, strength, and balance while reducing stress and improving focus and sleep quality. Yoga sessions typically include warm-up exercises, guided postures, controlled breathing, meditation, and cool-down relaxation, creating a safe and holistic training experience for both body and mind.
